# ReadyCap Product Brief

ReadyCap is an independent SaaS platform for preparing projects that seek debt, equity or mixed financing.

The product turns scattered project information into a professional investment package for human review and later presentation to banks, funds, family offices, SOFOMs, development banks or other financing entities.

ReadyCap is not positioned as a simple AI document generator. It is infrastructure for origination, structuring, document validation and financial readiness.

## Sprint 1 Principle

Build the secure operating base first:

- Multi-tenant data separation
- Supabase Auth and RLS
- Organizations, clients and projects
- Required-block checklist
- Source documents
- Simulated deliverables
- Audit trail

AI, real document generation, Excel generation, payments and white-labeling are explicitly out of scope for Sprint 1.
